This site is intended for accredited investors as defined under SEC Rule 501 of Regulation D. Any securities offering is made only through a sponsor’s confidential private placement memorandum (PPM) and related documents, which contain the complete terms, fees, conflicts, and risk factors, and only after a suitability determination. In the event of any conflict between this site and a PPM, the PPM controls.

DST, QOF, and related real-estate securities are speculative and illiquid and involve substantial risk, including:

  • possible loss of some or all principal;
  • illiquidity and the absence of a public market for the interests;
  • lack of investor control over management and disposition;
  • use of leverage, which can magnify losses;
  • dependence on the sponsor’s execution and on real-estate market conditions; and
  • the risk that a 1031 exchange fails to qualify for tax deferral.
