# Baker 1031 — DST & 1031 Exchange Advisors

> Baker 1031 is a Delaware Statutory Trust (DST) and 1031 exchange brokerage that helps accredited investors defer capital gains and own institutional real estate passively — through DSTs, 1031 and 721 (UPREIT) exchanges, Opportunity Zone funds, mineral & royalty interests, and REITs.

Baker 1031 operates an independent DST offerings marketplace, a directory of 82 tracked sponsors with realized full-cycle track records, a market-data center, and an educational insights library. Securities offered through Aurora Securities, Inc. (member FINRA/SIPC). Content is informational only and is not an offer to sell or a solicitation to buy any security, or tax or legal advice.

Full text of all strategies and the complete insights library (concatenated for AI ingestion) is available at {{BASE}}/llms-full.txt
