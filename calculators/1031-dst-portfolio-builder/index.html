How the Investment Finder works

This tool sketches an illustrative diversified DST allocation from your exchange amount and objective. Income-and-stability tilts toward net-lease, medical, and manufactured housing; growth tilts toward multifamily, industrial, and self-storage. It is a starting point for a conversation — not a recommendation or an offer. When you are ready, we build an actual portfolio from current, available offerings matched to your exchange, debt, and timeline.

Frequently asked questions

Is this a recommendation?
No. The output is an educational illustration to frame a conversation. Any actual investment is offered only through a sponsor's private placement memorandum after a suitability review.
Why diversify across several DSTs?
Spreading an exchange across property types and sponsors can reduce single-asset and single-tenant concentration. Diversification does not guarantee a profit or protect against loss.
How many DSTs will I actually hold?
It depends on your exchange amount, minimums, and debt-replacement needs. The estimate here is a rough guide; we tailor the actual number to available offerings.
